Demographics & Statistics

  • Population: Approximately 3.6 million (2021 estimate)
  • Area: 1,769 square kilometers
  • Density: About 2,030 people per square kilometer
  • Major Ethnic Groups: Gwari, Hausa, and others
  • Predominant Religion: Islam and Christianity
  • Languages Spoken: English (official), Gwari, Hausa, and other local languages
  • Climate: Tropical savanna climate
  • Educational Institutions: University of Abuja, Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University, etc.

Events

  • Abuja Arts and Culture Festival: Celebrating local art and culture, scheduled for December 15, 2023.
  • Nigeria International Trade Fair: Showcasing local businesses and promoting trade, taking place December 10-20, 2023.
  • Christmas Carol Concert: Community event featuring local choirs, happening on December 24, 2023.
  • New Year Bash: Celebrating the New Year with fireworks and festivities in the city center, January 1, 2024.
